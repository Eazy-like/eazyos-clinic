1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Eazy.OS Clinic (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses and protects information when you use eazyosclinic.com or contact us.

2. Information we may collect

  • Contact details you send us (name, email, and any message content).
  • Booking or payment-related details handled by third-party providers (e.g. PayPal).
  • Basic technical information such as IP address, browser type and pages visited (if analytics tools are enabled).

3. How we use your information

  • To reply to enquiries and deliver our services.
  • To manage bookings, payments and session communication.
  • To improve and protect our website and services.

4. Payments & third-party services

Payments are processed by third-party providers (e.g. PayPal). We do not store your full card details on our systems. Their privacy policies apply to any information you provide directly to them.

5. How we store and protect data

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we aim to keep your data safe.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary to deliver our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct or delete your personal data. To make a request, contact us using the email below.

8. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, email us at support@eazyosclinic.com.
